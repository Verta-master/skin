About Us

Skiing is a combination of raw materials derived from nature, the power of science, and know-how of manufacturing technology over a long period of time.

We minimize the sales channel to shorten the usage period (100 days from the manufacturing date / 5 months when refrigerated), and make sure that the product is shipped. The compact size of the recyclable container unifies the lifetime of the consumer, travel, and exercise in everyday life.

SKINOGRAPHY is implementing the 'elimination of the use of any chemical preservative' that is difficult for large manufacturers to realize, Unlike some small natural cosmetics manufacturers that simply blend just the right ingredients. By applying ingredient selection and compounding know-how accumulated through many years of R & D to products, we realized "good raw material + optimal function" natural shampoo / cosmetic concept.

SKINOGRAPH shampoo is a functional shampoo which is harmless to human body by using natural derived surfactant, excluding the use of any silicone, paraben and chemical preservative. SKINOGRAPHY PHARMACEUTICALS is a product that combines the accumulated know-how of manufacturing technology and ingredients with years of accumulated experience of CEO. We will separate the regeneration line and the water line to broaden the choice of consumers according to the skin characteristics.
